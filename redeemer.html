Crossroads Campus Ministries

Crossroads Campus ministries is an on campus ministry at UCSD that I am actively involved in. Our vision statement is “Pointing Collegians to the Cross”. My involvment thus far has been logo design for their annual sweaters.

Although branding is not something I am particularly interested in pursuing, it was a good experience and good practice thinking about purposeful design. Logo design has taught me to think of the target audience, and to include relevant elements all while keeping it simple enough for viewers to recognize at first glance and complex enough to not miss anything.
